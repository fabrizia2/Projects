Biography

Storytelling is my heartbeat, my passion, my purpose. Whether I’m stepping into a role on screen, breathing life into words behind a microphone, or sparking powerful conversations on air, I bring authenticity, energy, and depth to every project I touch.

As an actress, I don’t just play characters—I become them. Every role is a journey, every scene a chance to stir emotions and leave an impact. I thrive in the art of transformation, embodying stories that resonate and inspire.

As a voiceover artist, my voice carries worlds. It tells stories, sells ideas, and connects deeply with audiences. Whether it’s the warmth of a commercial, the depth of an audiobook, or the magic of an animated character, I bring words to life with tone, texture, and soul.

As a talk show host, I live for connection. I create space for real conversations, engaging minds, and amplifying voices that need to be heard. Whether it’s deep discussions or lighthearted moments, I bring energy and authenticity to every interaction.
